# Wolf reaper art

Drop your wolf-reaper images here so the app uses them:

- **`public/reaper.png`** — the reaper avatar shown everywhere (onboarding, the
  countdown hero, and the Death dock). The **circular bust portrait** works best
  (square crop, the wolf's face centered). Shown inside a circular ember-lit
  frame, so a square image with the face centered looks great.

- **`public/reaper-full.png`** *(optional)* — a full-body wolf reaper for use as
  a faded background (not wired up yet — tell me if you want it as the
  onboarding/dashboard backdrop and I'll add it).

Until `reaper.png` exists, the app falls back to the original hand-drawn SVG
reaper automatically — nothing breaks.

PNG or JPG both work (keep the filename `reaper.png` either way, or tell me the
name you used). A ~512×512 square is plenty.
